There are 196 countries. But, perhaps more importantly, there is a flag for each one of them.

Spanning all sorts of colors, shapes, and sizes, this project is a visualization of all the ways lands and heritages are defined within their respective rectangles. And triangles if you do your own thing like Nepal.

Here, elements like crescents, stars, stripes, and multiple colors can all co-exist. The Flag Pattern Maker puts asides our cultural and societal differences, and instead shows us what really unites (and divides) us as a flag-loving species.
